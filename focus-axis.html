Focus Axis Live

Focus Axis is a specialized application designed as a Jackson Cross Cylinder (JCC) simulator. It provides an interactive, digital way to practice and improve your skills in this crucial refractive assessment technique.

Key Features:

  • Interactive Refraction: Practice JCC subjective refraction by inputting trial lens values and simulating patient responses.
  • Realistic Simulation: Experience JCC axis and power refinement modes with adjustable rotation increments.
  • Visual Feedback: See a graphical representation of the eye, trial lenses, and JCC orientation. Simulated visual acuity provides a realistic feel.
  • Performance Tracking: Compare your trial lens results to a hidden patient prescription, track remaining error, and calculate spherical equivalent.
  • Practice Generator: Generate random patient prescriptions for endless practice scenarios.
  • Guided Learning: Built-in instructions help you understand and follow the JCC procedure.

Focus Axis is a valuable tool for students and practitioners to practice and hone their skills in subjective refraction using the Jackson Cross Cylinder technique in a controlled, interactive environment with immediate feedback.
