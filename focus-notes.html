Focus Notes Upcoming

Focus Notes is an upcoming marketplace where the Focus team and OptoBharat will offer high-quality study notes tailored to the new optometry curriculum. Designed for students and professionals, this platform aims to provide accessible and relevant educational content.

Key Features & Purpose:

  • Curriculum-Aligned Notes: Access study materials specifically created or curated to match the latest optometry curriculum.
  • Contributor Marketplace: A platform for the Focus team and OptoBharat to provide trusted educational resources.
  • Accessible Learning: Easily find and acquire comprehensive notes to support your studies and professional development.
  • Structured Content: Notes designed for clarity and ease of understanding.

Development Status:

Focus Notes is currently under development as a marketplace platform. We are working on the structure and content delivery system to provide valuable resources to the optometry community.
